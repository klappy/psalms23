🏆 Recommended Approach

After evaluating all three options, Option 1: The Shepherd's Journey is the recommended approach for the following reasons:

Strengths

  • Intuitive vertical scrolling
  • Strong narrative flow
  • Technically feasible
  • Mobile-optimized
  • Scalable for enhancements
  • Universal accessibility

Considerations

  • Less interactive than other options
  • May feel familiar (scrolling)
  • Requires careful performance optimization

This approach provides the best balance of visual impact, user experience, and technical feasibility while maintaining the contemplative nature of Psalm 23.
